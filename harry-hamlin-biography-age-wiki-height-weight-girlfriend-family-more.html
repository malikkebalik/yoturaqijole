As of his career, he made his television debut in the 1976 production of Taming of the Shrew, and in 1979 he appeared in a lead role in the television miniseries, Studs Lonigan. In 1984, he appeared in Master of the Game Space 1985. In the legal drama series, L.A. Law, he played the lead role as attorney Michael Kuzak.

From 1986-1991, he appeared in this series and was nominated for three Golden Globe Awards for Best Actor. From 2013-2014, he appeared in the role of Jim Cutler, in his major television work is Mad Men and also nominated for Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Guest Actor in this series.

From 1999 to 2000, he appeared in the show Movies Stars as Reese Hardin. After that, he appeared in several series such as Veronica Mars (2004-06), Shameless (2012-14), and Army Wives (2010-11).
