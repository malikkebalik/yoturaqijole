Ana de Armas is best known for her appearances in the films Knives Out and Knock Knock. Here’s a look at de Armas’ net worth, career, and how she became famous.

Ana de Armas’ movies and TV shows

Ana de Armas made her film acting debut in the 2006 movie Virgin Rose. She played the role of Marie. The following year she appeared in the film Madrigal. The actress landed her first recurring role when she starred in the television series The Boarding School. She played the character Carolina Leal Solís from 2007 to 2010.

Her first English movie role was in the 2015 film Knock Knock, alongside actor Keanu Reeves. After that, she appeared in the film Anabel. She began playing bigger roles in the films War Dogs, Bladerunner 2049, and Knives Out.

Ana de Armas’ highest-grossing films

As of this writing, de Armas’ highest-grossing film is Knives Out, with more than $305 million in worldwide box office earnings. This includes more than $165.3 million in domestic earnings and more than $140.3 million at the international box office. Her other high-grossing films are War Dogs, with more than $86.2 million in worldwide box office earnings; Yesterday, with more than $153 million in worldwide earnings; and Bladerunner 2049, with more than $259 million in worldwide box office earnings.

Ana de Armas had to overcome a major hurdle

Ana de Armas had to overcome a language barrier before she could fully jump into making movies in English. During a 2016 interview with the BUILD series, the actress revealed she didn’t know English when she first moved to Los Angeles.

“When I moved to LA two years ago, I didn’t speak English at all,” says de Armas. Knock Knock was her first English-language movie. When the host asked her what gave her the confidence to move to Los Angeles to start an acting career without being able to speak English, de Armas simply says she loves a challenge.“I like adventure and challenge,” said de Armas.

Ana de Armas’ net worth

As of this writing, Ana de Armas has a net worth of $4 million according to Celebrity Net Worth estimates.

What’s next for Ana de Armas

This will be a very busy year for de Armas. In February 2020, she appeared in the film The Night Clerk, alongside actor Tye Sheridan. In April, her film Sergio was released. Later this year, de Armas is set to appear in No Time to Die, Deep Water, and Blonde.
